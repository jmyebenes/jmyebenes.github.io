Privacy Policy

At MonkyDev, we value your privacy and want to be transparent about how data is managed in our apps.

1. Data we collect

Our apps only collects usage data and crash reports through Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics. This includes:

  • Information on how the app is used (visited screens, events).
  • Crash reports and technical errors.

2. Purpose of data collection

This data is used solely to:

  • Improve the functionality and performance of the app.
  • Detect and resolve errors and crashes.
  • Analyze general app usage for future improvements.

3. Data sharing

Collected data is sent to Firebase (Google LLC) for analysis. It is not shared with advertisers or third parties outside this service.

4. Data security

All data sent to Firebase is transmitted encrypted, ensuring secure transfer.

5. Consent and control

Collecting this data is necessary for the proper functioning of the app and cannot be disabled. No personally identifiable user data is collected.

6. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy at any time. Please review it periodically to stay informed of any changes.
